     About us

    A typing test is designed to find how fast one types in a given amount of time. We designed a typometer website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP & Bootstrap that presents simple typing challenge and finds the performance of typing by calculating the Words Per Minute (WPM) and the accuracy of the typed characters.

    The website shows a series of paragraphs that have to be typed in a specific time limit, as fast as possible. A higher typing speed would show a higher WPM value. Incorrectly typed letters would be marked accordingly during typing. By calculating correct words it shows accuracy.
